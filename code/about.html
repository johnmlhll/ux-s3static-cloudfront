About John

This page is about my general background to date covering three careers. A more detailed review of my work history is available on my Resume page. If you wish to know more detail about me and my career plans, please reach out on Contact page, and I will be happy to supply my Curriculum Vitae with any answers to your questions.

Through The Years

A native of Dublin Ireland, my early days were spent in Dublin with summers at my mother's home farm in Co Cavan, where I learned about a good work ethic, healthy living and Ireland's living history. After secondary school and a few years of private security, I joined the Irish Defence Forces, where my developing work ethic and interest saw me been transformed into a more desciplined and focused version of myself.

My interest in writing and aptitude for numbers saw me guided into Financial Operations after the Military where I qualified as a banker with the Certificate in Financial Services from University College Dublin and the Institute of Banking. My ongoing interest in technology and project management allowed me to gain good project management experience in systems during my 12 years of leadership in Financial Operations. This led me to 2012 when my redundancy from Integra Lifesciences sparked a revival of what I hold dear, which is Software Development and watching Dublin win the All Ireland GAA Finals! Hopefully this portion of my site for some time to come!!... #COYBIB

After reflecting on my deep passion for consultative leadership and clan-adhocracy company culture, I began to plan my future in terms of pursuit of happiness and passion for the work I do. This brought me down a planned road where milestones were complimented by self discovery and contentment on the journey that has brought me to Software. This continuous journey is marked by the following milestones:

  • 2013-2014: Certificate in Advanced Project Management for the ICT Professional (QQI Level 8)
  • 2014-2015: Certificate in Software and Cloud Technology (QQI Level 8)
  • 2015-2016: Internship in Big Data Analysis and Marketing at Sonra Intelligence Ltd
  • 2016-2017: Higher Diploma in Science in Computing(Software Development) (QQI Level 8)

The Level 8 (Hons Degree Strem) QQI awards were the result of my Study from 2013 at Dublin Business School (a.k.a. DBS) where I acheived 1st class honours in all 3rd level qualifications acheived!
